About the Event

The California Association of Asian Pacific Leaders in Education (CAAPLE) is hosting the organization’s inaugural symposium, “Lifting As We Climb: Deepening Representation For System Change" in Castro Valley, CA. The purpose of CAAPLE's symposium is to call into action and lift educational leaders to develop and advance professionally and build capacity within their schools, districts, and organizations to meet the needs of AAPI students and staff.
